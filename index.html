You're not alone

Anxiety-related behaviours are one of the most common challenges dog owners face.

Most owners don't know what triggers their dog, how to read the signs early, or what to actually do in the moment. This guide changes that.

😰

Destructive behaviour

Chewing furniture, scratching doors, toilet accidents — these are anxiety symptoms, not bad behaviour. They have solutions.

🌩️

Fear of storms & loud noises

Fireworks, thunder, traffic — overstimulation sends many dogs into a panic spiral that owners feel helpless to stop.

🏠

Separation distress

Howling, pacing, or self-harm the moment you leave. Separation anxiety is one of the most common — and most fixable — dog issues.
